## Generation Overview

### Original Xbox (2001)
**Microsoft's entry into the console market**
- **CPU:** 733 MHz Intel Pentium III
- **Key Innovation:** Built-in hard drive
- **Flagship Title:** Halo: Combat Evolved
- **Units Sold:** 24 million

### Xbox 360 (2005)
**Introduced Xbox Live and achievements**
- **CPU:** 3.2 GHz PowerPC
- **Key Features:** Xbox Live Arcade, Gamerscore
- **Best-Selling Game:** Kinect Adventures
- **Units Sold:** 84 million

### Xbox One (2013)
**Focus on entertainment ecosystem**
- **CPU:** 1.75 GHz AMD Jaguar
- **Key Features:** HDMI passthrough, Kinect 2.0
- **Notable Exclusive:** Sunset Overdrive
- **Units Sold:** 58 million

### Xbox Series X/S (2020)
**Current generation powerhouses**
- **CPU:** 3.8 GHz Zen 2
- **Key Features:** Quick Resume, Smart Delivery
- **Flagship Title:** Halo Infinite
- **Units Sold:** 21 million (and counting)
